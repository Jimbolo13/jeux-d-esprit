✨ Jours étranges ✨

Welcome, Valeriya.

I’ve been learning how to code lately, and I started building small interactive things like this.

This one is for you. Nothing serious. Nothing to solve. Just something to wander through.

Maybe it will make you smile. Maybe it will distract you for a moment. Maybe it will just exist — and that’s already enough.

You will make choices. They don’t have to be logical. They don’t have to make sense.

Just follow the feeling.
